About OnionTools

Welcome to OnionTools, a team of dedicated individuals working towards developing privacy software for desktop operating systems. Our aim is to empower individuals and organizations with the tools they need to protect their online privacy and security.

Our Vision

At OnionTools, we believe that privacy is a fundamental human right. In today's digital age, where data breaches and surveillance have become all too common, it is essential to have reliable and user-friendly software that can safeguard sensitive information.

What We Do

We specialize in developing privacy-focused software for desktop operating systems, including Windows, macOS, and Linux. Our team of experienced developers, security experts, and privacy advocates work tirelessly to create tools that offer robust encryption, anonymous browsing, secure communication channels, and more.
